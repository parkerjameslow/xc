      Paste a full meet's results

      Paste a whole race from SportTrax/Athletic.net (copy the text, not a link). Runners already in the app get that race logged — their projections update and the board re-ranks. Anyone new gets added. Set the course difficulty once and it applies to the whole field.

      Log a 2026 race result

      Add a runner

      Two uses. Complete a real returner who finished below last year's top 50 (Lehi's 4th–7th, etc.): give her 2025 place, time, and grade and she becomes a true returner with an up/down arrow — and takes a “proj JV” slot on her team. Or promote a JV runner into varsity: leave the 2025 fields blank and just give a projected time. Tag JV promotes in the name, e.g. "Camille Shields (6th runner)".
