      Add a runner

      Two uses. Complete a real returner who finished below last year's top 50 (Lehi's 4th–7th, etc.): give her 2025 place, time, and grade and she becomes a true returner with an up/down arrow — and takes a “proj JV” slot on her team. Or promote a JV runner into varsity: leave the 2025 fields blank and just give a projected time. Tag JV promotes in the name, e.g. "Camille Shields (6th runner)".

      Logged this season

      How the projection works. Every athlete's grade from the 2025 state results decides who returns: seniors are removed, everyone else moves up a class. Projected time starts from her 2025 time minus a standard class improvement (freshman→soph −25s, soph→junior −18s, junior→senior −12s). Once you log 2026 races, that runner's projection switches to her actual recent form and the whole board re-ranks. '26 place is her rank among returners + anyone you add; the move chip compares it to her actual 2025 place.

      Caveats. Baseline is the full 2025 6A girls state field (all 117 finishers). Where a team graduated enough runners to leave fewer than seven returners, the gaps are filled with projected JV runners (tagged and asterisked) until you enter the real call-ups. Times aren't course-adjusted, so compare 2026 marks sensibly (a hilly course will look "slow"). Realignment or transfers aren't accounted for automatically — add or remove runners as needed. Projections are estimates, not predictions.

      Data: 2025 UHSAA 6A Girls State Championship results (Deseret News / UHSAA). Built as a personal planning tool.
